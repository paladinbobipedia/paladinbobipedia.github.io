# Tern

*A hymn unto Tern, celestial avian knight, whose wings bespeak dominion o'er the skies, a beacon of valor and grace, forever soaring in realms divine.*

## Lore and Origin

Tern, oft hailed as the "Sky-Strider" or "Azure Warden," doth originate from whispered legends spun 'midst the hallowed halls of ancient orders. Some pious scribes claim Tern sprang forth from the first sunrise, a paragon of purity born with the dawn's golden light, imbued with the very essence of righteous flight. Others posit a divine decree,  forged by the hand of a celestial architect, tasked to safeguard the heavens from encroaching shadows. Regardless its genesis, Tern's existence is interwoven with the fabric of cosmic balance, a vigilant protector against the unhallowed.

## Physiognomy and Abilities

Behold, Tern! A creature of majestic silhouette, bearing the plumage of a tempestuous twilight, feathers shifting 'twixt sapphire and amethyst. Eyes like molten gold pierce the veil of mortals, glimmering with ancient wisdom. Its wings, vast as storm clouds, cleave the heavens with effortless grace, empowered by a celestial wind  alone known to its kind. Tern wields not mundane weaponry, but the very forces of air and light.  Sunbeams coalesce into lances of radiant energy, whilst howling gales become its blades, slicing asunder the wicked's machinations.

## Role as Protector

Tern serves as a bastion against the insidious creep of darkness. It patrols the ethereal planes, a vigilant sentinel against malevolent entities seeking to breach our world.  Legends speak of Tern intercepting corrupted souls, guiding them back to the light, or vanquishing daemons with righteous fury. Its presence, a shimmering herald of hope, inspires valor in the hearts of beleaguered heroes and instills dread in the wicked, a constant reminder that even amidst twilight, justice takes flight.

## Encounters and Reverence

Sighting Tern is deemed a hallowed omen, a testament to enduring good in a world oft shrouded in shadow. While seldom landing amongst mortals, its aerial ballet inspires awe and fervent prayer. Knights and pilgrims alike carve its image upon shields and banners, seeking a sliver of its protective aegis. To encounter Tern directly is considered a grace surpassing earthly reward, a vision whispering of unwavering faith and the triumph of  light over encroaching night.
