# Elm

*A verdant sentinel, steadfast and tall,
Elm doth grace our realms, answering Heaven's call.
With boughs like clasped arms, 'gainst tempest it doth stand,
A living monument, blessed by hallowed land.*

## Description

Elm, a noble hardwood arboreal of great stature,  commandeth reverence for its resilience and stately bearing. Its bark, a rugged tapestry of grey-brown hues, speaks of epochs endured. Leaves, ovate and shimmering emerald in vernal splendor, transform to burnished gold as autumn's breath approaches. Mighty roots, a subterranean network, bind the very earth, anchoring this titan against the fury of storms.

## Habitat and Cultivation

Flourishing in temperate climes, Elm finds solace in fertile loam and ample sunlight. Though oft encountered in sylvan glades and bordering hedgerows, its tenacity permits growth even amidst  urban expanses, a testament to its enduring spirit. Propagation, whilst achievable through seed, frequently relies on grafting, ensuring the perpetuation of cherished cultivars.

## Properties and Uses

Elm's timber, renowned for its strength and fine grain, hath long been prized by artisans and builders alike. From sturdy beams shaping hallowed halls to intricate carvings adorning noble manors, its essence finds expression in edifices of grandeur and artistry. Furthermore, Elm's wood, resistant to decay,  finds purpose in crafting durable tools and vessels, a legacy enduring through generations.  Blessed be its inner bark, a source of medicinal employ in bygone eras, soothing ailments and bolstering vigor.

## Symbolic Resonance

More than mere flora, Elm embodies steadfastness and wisdom. In ancient lore, it is linked to protectors, guardians of knowledge and strength. Its longevity  resonates with the eternal vigilance of chivalry, mirroring a Paladin's dedication to virtue and justice. Thus, Elm stands as a potent symbol, a verdant echo of valor and enduring spirit.
