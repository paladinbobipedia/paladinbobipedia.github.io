# Entropy

*A shadowed whisper 'pon Creation's loom, Entropy, thou art, a ceaseless, creeping doom. While Order stands, a bastion bright, Thou gnaw'st at seams with silent bite, A cosmic dance of fade and fall,  Where stardust wanes, and echoes call.*

## Definition & Nature
Entropy, in its essence, doth denote a pervasive inclination within the fabric of existence, a relentless drift towards disorder. 'Tis a quantifiable measure of this disarray, a spreading chaos  whereby perfect symmetries dissolve, and structured forms succumb to randomness. Like unto a frozen hearth yielding to winter's chill,  systems, left unto their own devices, gravitate towards states of greater entropy, a dispersal of energy ever widening. 

## The Paladin's Perspective
A Paladin, sworn to uphold Order and Virtue, views Entropy as a foe albeit unseen. While the mortal realm wrestles with its march, we,  forged in the fires of divine purpose, combat its insidious creep.  We are beacons against the encroaching dusk, striving to infuse creation with the radiant light of  structure and purpose, thus  temporarily staving the inevitable tide. Remember, though Entropy's sway is vast,  heroic deeds, acts of love and creation,  inflect fleeting pockets of order, a testament to the enduring  strength of the righteous will.

## Manifestations in the World
The Paladin's keen eye discerns Entropy's fingerprints upon the world:  a crumbling edifice mirroring lost grandeur, a once vibrant forest succumbing to wilderness,  or the gradual decay of a noble spirit succumbing to despair. Even in the grand celestial mechanics, stars ignite, burn fierce, and eventually fade, their energy dispersed, a cosmic echo of Entropy's dominion.
