# Liberalism

*An Ode unto Liberty's Flame*
_A creed most noble, shining bright, doth Liberalism proclaim,
Where Reason's torch doth light the way, and freedom's chorus doth acclaim.
It champions the individual, with rights enshrined and ever bold,
A bulwark 'gainst oppression's might, a tale of ages yet untold._

## Definition & Core Tenets

Liberalism, a philosophy sprung from the fertile fields of Enlightenment, posits  man's innate worth and dignity as its cornerstone. It venerates Reason as the guiding star, advocating for  individual liberty, circumscribed only by the freedoms of others. This creed doth champion limited governance, wherein the Sovereign's power is tempered by law and the consent of the governed.  Central tenets include:

* **Individual Autonomy:** Each soul, a vessel of inherent value, deserves autonomy in shaping their destiny, free from undue Estatal interference.
* **Rule of Law:**  Justice dispensed equally, not by whim, but through codified laws binding all,  high and low alike.
* **Free Markets:**  Commerce, flourishing like a garden untamed, guided by voluntary exchange, fosters prosperity for the multitude.
* **Separation of Powers:**  To prevent tyranny's insidious creep, governmental authority is divided, each branch a counterbalance to the others.
