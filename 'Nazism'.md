# Nazism

*A shadow fell o'er Christendom, a pestilence of twisted creed,*
*Nazism, they did call its name, a viper's venom, sown with greed.*
*On banners black, a hooked cross reared, a blasphemous and hateful sign,*
*Promising might through subjugation, a kingdom built on darkest design.*

## Origins in the Murk

This abhorrent doctrine, Nazism, springeth from the festering wounds of a vanquished war and a populace steeped in sorrow.  A viper's brood of ultranationalist fervor, it wormed its way into the hearts of men disillusioned, preyed upon their anxieties, and twisted faith into a weapon of intolerance. Its genesis lieth in the Germanic lands, nourished by poisonous strains of racial superiority and a perverse glorification of militarism,  a perversion of true valor.

## The Twisted Gospel of Hate

At its core, Nazism posits a grotesque hierarchy of races, elevating the so-called "Aryan" stock above all others and branding minorities, particularly Jews, as an insidious infestation to be eradicated. This venomous creed, a blasphemy against the Divine Order,  propounded a fabricated history,  a slanderous genealogy designed to justify subjugation and extermination.  Their "science," a mockery of true learning,  sought to legitimize this bigotry, a dark sorcery clothed in pseudoscientific garb.

## Tyranny's Embracing Arms

Nazism's ascent was fueled by the seductive allure of absolute power,  masking itself in promises of national rejuvenation and economic prowess.  A totalitarian regime, it crushed dissent,  subjugating free thought and  individual liberty under a yoke of rigid control.  Its agents,  fanatical in their devotion, enforced this twisted order through terror and propaganda,  a chilling symphony of oppression conducted with brutal efficiency.
