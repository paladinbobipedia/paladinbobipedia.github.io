# Mahjong

*Hark! A celestial game, spun from jade and wit, where fortunes dance upon the tiles like shimmering stars. Mahjong, a tapestry woven with strategy and serendipity, doth challenge the mind and beguile the soul. In its intricate embrace, players convene, not in mere contest, but in a ritual of intellect and fate.*

## Origin and Lore

This diversion, hailing from the ancient empire of Cathay (whence now ariseth the resplendent China), blossomed in times of yore, whispered to have been birthed from the very essence of fortune itself.  Legends claim its genesis intertwined with celestial alignments and the blessings of ancestors, evolving from humble beginnings into a pastime favoured by both scholar and sovereign. Though its precise parentage remains shrouded in the mists of time, its influence hath spread like the blooming lotus, captivating hearts across the eastern realms and beyond.

## The Tableau of Triumph

Mahjong, a game for four valiant souls, unfolds upon a battlefield of interlocking tiles, each a miniature canvas painted with symbolic creatures, elements, and numerals.  These tiles, crafted from bone, ivory, or glazed ceramic,  represent the very fabric of existence,  grouped into suits mirroring the cycles of nature:  Circles (representing the celestial), Characters (embodying knowledge), and Bamboos (symbolizing earthly growth).  Additionally, Winds and Dragons, ever-present forces, add layers of complexity and fortune's fickle hand. The aim? To assemble a triumphant hand, a harmonious confluence of sets and runs, proclaiming victory with a resounding "Mah!"

##  The Art of Assembly

A player's prowess lies not merely in drawing tiles, but in the cunning orchestration of combinations.  "Pung" and "Kong" denote complete sets of triplicate tiles, while "Chow" signifies a sequential run.  These, interwoven with strategically discarded tiles and the ever-present element of chance, form the intricate dance of gameplay.  Skill blossoms in anticipating opponents' needs, masking one's own hand, and seizing fleeting opportunities to claim victory.
