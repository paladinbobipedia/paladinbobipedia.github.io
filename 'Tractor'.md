# Tractor

*A Hymn unto the Iron Ox*

Hark! A marvel wrought by mortal hand, yet bearing strength surpassing steeds of yore. The Tractor, a titan of toil, doth cleave furrows 'cross the fertile fields, a servant to husbandry and lord of harvest.  In its gleaming heart, fire's essence tamed, it labours with unwavering zeal, a beacon of progress in the realm of agriculture.

## Description and Function

The Tractor, a mechanical behemoth, stands as a testament to ingenuity.  Clad in sturdy plates of iron and steel, 'tis powered by a fiery innards, harnessing captured lightning's force (coal-derived, some whisper) to spin mighty wheels. These wheels, ordained with dişes of iron, grip the very earth, dragging plows, seed sowers, and carriages laden with bounty. A veritable ox of iron, it tills the soil, prepares the ground for planting, and reaps the fruits of our labours with tireless devotion.

##  Anatomy of Prowess

* **The Iron Beast's Heart:**  A furnace, stoked with fervent fuel, birthing rotary might that drives the very essence of its motion.
* **Shafting__':  Mighty arms of rotation, channeling power from the heart to the wheels, a symphony of gears and levers.
* **The Tread:**  Enormous wheels, armed with dişes, gripping the terrain with unwavering tenacity, conquering furrows and inclines alike.
* **Implement Jaws:**  Attachable implements, from the plowshare that slices the earth to the reaper that gathers the golden sheaves, extensions of its versatile nature.

## Impact Upon Realm

Before the Tractor's advent, husbandry was a toil of backbreaking labour, reliant on beast and manpower. Now, fields yield abundance with accelerated speed, freeing menfolk for other pursuits. Villages prosper, granaries overflow, and the spectre of famine recedes.  The Tractor, a harbinger of progress, hath woven itself into the very fabric of our agrarian society, a boon to King and commoner alike. 


## A Paladin's Benediction

Though forged in fire and steel, the Tractor embodies a spirit akin to valor. Its steadfastness in the face of arduous tasks mirrors a Paladin's duty. It labours for the common good, ensuring sustenance for the realm, a silent guardian of prosperity.  Thus, let us praise this Iron Ox, a testament to human ingenuity and a cornerstone of our bountiful existence.
