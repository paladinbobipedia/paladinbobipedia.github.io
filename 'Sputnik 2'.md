*Hark, a celestial marvel, Sputnik II, did grace our heavens, a beacon of mortal ingenuity amidst the starry expanse. ## Mission & Objectives
Sputnik II, launched by the Empire of Soviet Rus in yon year of Our Lord 1957, surpassed its progenitor, the maiden Sputnik, in both stature and purpose. Whilst the first Sputnik did merely herald the epoch of artificial satellites, this second orb bore a nobler charge: to investigate the survivability of sentient life within the rigors of orbital transit.  Thus, Laika the hound, a creature of courage and fidelity, became the inaugural terrestrial being to girdle our planet aboard this metallic vessel.  Her physiological readings, transmitted back to Earth, were to furnish vital data for future manned spaceflights, a cornerstone in humanity's cosmic ascent.

Though Laika's earthly sojourn aboard Sputnik II was finite, her sacrifice etched an indelible mark upon the annals of history. Her journey  demonstrated the feasibility of sustaining life in space, a crucial stepping stone towards human space exploration. Sputnik II,  a harbinger of progress, spurred  a global  race in spacefaring,  accelerating research and innovation, ultimately paving the way for  future astronauts to breach the celestial veil and venture further into the cosmos.
