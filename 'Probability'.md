# Probability

**An Ode to the Guiding Hand in Fortune's Dance**
*Probability, mistress veiled in mystic loom,*
*Where threads of chance in intricate patterns bloom.
Thou guidest not with edict, but with whispered sway,
A subtle current shaping Fate's winding way.*

## Definition & Essence

Probability, a conclave of wisdom ancient and profound, doth concern itself with the likelihood, or rather the *chance*,  of sundry occurrences unfolding within the grand tapestry of events. 'Tis not a decree of iron, but a  subtle calculus woven from observation and reason. We ponder the frequency of past happenings,  extracting thence a measure of how apt a given outcome *might* be in the hereafter. Thus, we speak not of certainty, but of degrees thereof, expressed in fractions or percentages, ever mindful that the Wheel of Fortune spins with capricious grace.

##  The Calculus of Chances

To quantify this elusive mistress, we employ tools forged in the fires of logic.  Consider a venture with multiple potential results. Probability assigns a numerical value, ranging from naught (impossibility absolute) to unity (assured occurrence), to each such result.  The sum of these values for *all* possibilities must ever equal unity, reflecting the completeness of the conceivable outcomes. This summation unveils the comparative likelihoods, revealing which paths are more probable than others.

##  From Dice to Destinies

The humble gaming die, with its numbered faces, serves as a microcosm of this principle. Each face holds a probability of 1/6 (one in six) for appearing. Yet, cast myriad times, patterns emerge,  approaching the theoretical average. Thus,  Probability, though governing the random,  reveals order in the long run,  a whisper of predictability amidst chaos. ## A Paladin's Perspective

A Paladin, though guided by Divine Purpose,  neverthless walks a world tinged with uncertainty. Probability becomes a tool, not to *control*  Fortune, but to *navigate*  her.  By understanding  the chances, we make more  *wiser* choices,  balancing daring  with prudence. A calculated risk, informed by Probability, is  a virtue,  not recklessness. Thus, this knowledge,  a gift  from  reason and  experience,  enhances not only our tactical acumen but also our steadfast faith in a  higher order,  even amidst the ever-turning wheel of fate.
