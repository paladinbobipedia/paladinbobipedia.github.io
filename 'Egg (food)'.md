# Egg (food)

*A humble orb, from fowl divine, a treasure born of feathered shrine.  In shell of pearl, a life encased, a promise whispered, nourishment embraced.  Oh, Egg, thou art a gift most rare, sustenance and magic, beyond compare!*

## Provenance and Husbandry

Eggs, hallowed morsels, derive their being from the avian brood, creatures blessed by the celestial forge to lay these vessels of nascent life. Hens, commonly known as galluses,  within their warm cotes, nurture these fragile spheres, each a testament to nature's fecundity. Through diligent husbandry, tending to their comfort and feed, man procureth these gifts, a bounty born of feathered grace.

## Composition and Properties

This oval wonder, a marvel of alchemy, doth contain a symphony of elements vital to mortal sustenance. A proteinaceous heart, rich in the building blocks of flesh and vigor, it boasts fats essential for hearth and limb. Vitamins, like shimmering jewels,  cluster therein -  A, D, and B throngs,  each a spark of health untold. Minerals, too, find refuge in its shell-kissed embrace, calcium for bone and iron for the blood's red tide.

## Culinary Versatility

Most versatile is this gift from the fowl. Raw, a delicate indulgence for the daring palate, whisked into frothy clouds for cakes and custards divine. Boiled, its creamy yolk a sunburst, perfect with crusty bread. Fried, a golden halo 'pon sizzling fat,  a breakfast fit for kings. Baked in pies and quiches, it lends richness and substance.  Indeed, the kitchen's artistry knows no bounds when wielding the humble Egg.

## Symbolic Resonance

Beyond its corporeal sustenance, the Egg resonates with potent symbolism.  A genesis, a nascent potential, mirroring the birth of life itself. In many a faith, it signifies resurrection and renewal, a timeless echo of hope reborn. Thus, the Egg, a simple food, carries within its shell a universe of meaning.
