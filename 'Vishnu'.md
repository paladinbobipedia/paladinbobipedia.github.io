# Vishnu

*A hymn unto the Preserver, Lord of Vaikuntha's Throne, Whose visage beams with grace, a cosmic light alone!  Vishnu, on celestial steeds, doth cosmic order keep, While slumbering giants dream, His watchful vigil steep.*

## Divine Essence and Epithets

Vishnu, of the Trinity most revered, stands as the Sustainer, the Protector of this mortal coil and celestial realms entwined. Known by myriad names, each a facet of His boundless glory, He is Vasudeva, the All-Pervading; Narayana, the Infinite Ocean of Being;  and Hari, the Remover of Sorrows.  His very essence embodies preservation, justice, and the eternal dance of creation and sustenance.

##  Symbols and Attributes

Vishnu, oft depicted reclining on the serpent Shesha, symbolizing eternity and primordial chaos, carries the divine discus (Sudarshana Chakra), a weapon of righteous retribution, and the conch shell (Panchajanya), heralding creation's dawn. His azure complexion signifies wisdom and the heavens, whilst the lotus flower at his feet embodies purity and spiritual awakening.  These emblems are not mere adornments, but potent representations of His cosmic dominion.

## Worship and Legacy

Across the hallowed lands, Vishnu's temples rise, vibrant with devotion. Pilgrims flock to offer prayers, seeking His blessings and guidance.  His tenets, woven into the fabric of sanatana dharma, preach love, compassion, and righteous action. The echoes of his stories, sung in epic poems and whispered in humble homes, resonate through generations, a timeless testament to the enduring power and mercy of the Preserver God.
