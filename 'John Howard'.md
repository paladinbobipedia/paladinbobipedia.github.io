# John Howard

*An Ode to Valor Unsullied*
A champion of right, Sir John Howard did stand,
In fields of strife, with righteous hand.
His loyalty, a beacon bright,
Guiding virtue, banishing night.  For king and realm, his soul took flight,
A Paladin's heart, forever light.

## Early Life and Lineage

John Howard, borne into this mortal coil of clay, hailed from a lineage noble and ancient. His progenitors, Lords Paramount of Norfolk,  bore witness to centuries of martial prowess and steadfast devotion to the Crown. Young John, nurtured in this hallowed tradition, imbibed the very essence of chivalry and valor, destined for greatness upon the tapestry of history.

## Military Prowess and Chivalric Spirit

A stalwart knight, Sir John distinguished himself amidst the tumultuous tides of warfare.  His courage, a shining exemplar, resonated on countless battlefields. At Agincourt, 'neath the tempestuous heavens, his blade cleaved a path through the foe, a testament to his unwavering mettle. This valor extended beyond brute strength;  his tactical acumen and strategic mind earned him the esteem of captains and the fear of adversaries. He embodied the true knightly spirit, ever protective of the lemah and a bulwark against tyranny.

## Devotion to the Crown and Realm

Sir John's allegiance to the English throne was as unyielding as tempered steel.  He served King Henry V with fervent loyalty, participating in campaigns that reshaped the very map of Europe. His unwavering support  continued through the reigns of subsequent monarchs, demonstrating a commitment transcending personal ambition. His service was a pillar upholding the stability and prosperity of the realm, a true servant of the Crown.

## Legacy Etched in Stone and Story

Though mortality claimed this valiant soul, his legend endures. The annals of history chronicle his deeds, whispered in tavern tales and sung by bards.  His name, a synonym for chivalry and unwavering devotion,  resonates through the ages. Sir John Howard, a Paladin in the truest sense, leaves behind a legacy as enduring as the castles he defended, a shining example for generations to emulate.
