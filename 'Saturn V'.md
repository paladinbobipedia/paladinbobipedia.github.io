## Stages of Ascent: A Choreography of Power

1. **The First Stage (S-IC):** A colossus of fire, this basal segment, wielding twenty-one mighty RS-25 rocket motors, cleaved the bonds of gravity with a thunderous roar, propelling the noble craft skyward. Its fiery breath, a spectacle to behold, consumed tonnes of propellant, its liftoff shaking the very foundations of launch complexes.
2. **The Second Stage (S-II):** Upon the S-IC's spent pyre, this graceful successor ignited, its five J-2 engines a celestial choir,  guiding the ascent further into the heavens. A marvel of cryogenic fuel mastery, it nurtured the craft towards its orbital destiny.
3. **The Third Stage (S-IVB):**  A delicate dancer amidst titans, the S-IVB, powered by a single J-2 engine,  ushered the final, crucial leg of the journey. It injected the lunar module and command ship into their destined trajectories, a ballet of precision culminating in lunar embrace.
