# Mars

*Hark, O War-God stern, thy crimson visage bright,
A beacon 'midst the heavens, a warrior's guiding light.
From fields of conflict, thy baleful glory streams,
Mars, patron of valor,  fulfilling valiant dreams.*

## Astrological Connotations

Mars, the bellicose planet, doth hold dominion o'er martial affairs, courage, and passion's fervent fire. In celestial tapestry, he shines as a ruddy gem, associated with iron, blood, and the very essence of conflict. His sway extends to  ambition, energy, and the primal drive  that propels mankind to action, both in righteous battle and the pursuits of earthly conquest.

## Mythological Depiction

Within the hallowed halls of Roman legend, Mars stands as a titan, god of war in all its grim majesty.  A son of Jupiter, he embodies the organized fury of legions, the clash of steel, and the thunderous roar of siege engines. ## Celestial Orbit and Influence

Circling Sol at a distance fervent, Mars completes his celestial circuit in a span nigher to Earth's than any other. This proximity imbues him with potent influence upon our realm. Astrologers  decipher his position amongst the stars, believing his aspect  to  influence the tides of human endeavor,  kindling  boldness in hearts, stirring ambition, and  occasionally,  fanning the flames of discord.   Knights and commanders, heed his course, for in his movements lie whispers of fortune and peril on the battlefield.
