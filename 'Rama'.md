# Rama

*Hark, a hymn to Rama, Prince of Valor bold,
Whose righteous arm, like sunlight, stories doth unfold.
A King most just, with Sita by his side,
He vanquished evil,  with Truth as his guide.*

## Lineage and Avataric Descent

Rama, revered as the seventh avatar of the infinite Vishnu, sprang from the noble lineage of the Raghu dynasty.  Borne unto King Dasarath of Kosala, he was a prince of exemplary virtue, nurtured in the hallowed precepts of righteousness (dharma). His very birth was foretold, a beacon of hope to vanquish the demon-king Ravana's tyranny and restore cosmic balance.

## The Exemplary Prince

Rama's character shone with an ethereal radiance.  A paragon of chivalry, he embodied piety,  courage,  wisdom, and unwavering loyalty. His adherence to dharma was absolute, a guiding star in his every action. This sanctity earned him the adoration of his subjects and the respect even of his adversaries. Though destined for kingship, he chose exile, demonstrating  selflessness and obedience to familial duty, a testament to his profound moral compass.

## The Ramayana's Epic Tapestry

Rama's saga, etched in the timeless epic *Ramayana*,  unfolds a grand narrative of love,  duty, and the triumph of good over evil. His abduction of Sita by the demon Ravana ignited a righteous war. Leading his valiant army,  accompanied by the steadfast Hanuman and the noble Lakshmana,  he traversed treacherous landscapes and battled monstrous foes, ultimately rescuing his beloved queen and restoring order to the realm.

## Legacy of an Eternal Champion

Rama's influence transcends the confines of myth. He stands as a timeless symbol of righteousness,  a beacon illuminating the path to virtuous living. His teachings resonate through millennia, inspiring generations to uphold justice,  love unconditionally, and combat  iniquity with unwavering courage. His name,  a whispered prayer on countless lips,  forever echoes the victory of Dharma and the enduring power of a noble soul.
