# Rowan

*A hymn unto Rowan, bough of valor bright,*
*Whose spirit flames, a beacon in darkest night.  Loyal heart, 'neath Heaven's shield thou standest bold,
A whispered prayer, a story yet untold.*

## Lore and Ancestry

Rowan, a name etched upon the annals of valor, doth speak of an ancient lineage intertwined with the very fabric of heroism.  Whilst whispers trace their kin to nigh forgotten knights of yore, bound by oath to ancient protectors of the realm, concrete lineage remains shrouded in mists of time.  Yet, 'tis known they bear a bloodline touched by celestial grace, a heritage manifest in unyielding courage and a paladin's inherent righteousness.

## The Mark of Radiance

Distinguishable amongst mortals by a radiant mark upon their brow, resembling a nascent sunrise, Rowan bears the sigil of  profound spiritual attunement. This emblem, kindled in moments of valorous deed, glows brighter with each righteous act, serving as a visible testament to their unwavering devotion to justice and the sacred order. Some sages theorize this mark signifies a pact  with a celestial entity, though its precise nature remains a sacred secret, murmured only in hushed tones within hallowed halls.

##  Champion of the Faithful

Rowan walks a path consecrated to shielding the innocent and vanquishing encroaching darkness. A stalwart paladin, they wield both blade and faith as instruments of righteous might. Their prowess in combat is legendary, tempered by disciplined training and an inner fire fueled by unwavering belief. More than mere muscle, Rowan embodies compassion, oft acting as a beacon of hope for the downtrodden, a protector of the vulnerable, and a dispenser of solace in times of tribulation.  Their deeds resonate with echoes of ancient heroes, reminding all that true strength lies in service to a greater good.
