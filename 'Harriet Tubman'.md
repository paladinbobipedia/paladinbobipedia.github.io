# Harriet Tubman

*An hallowed soul, 'midst tempest's roar, she shone,
A beacon bright, on freedom's winding drone.
With dauntless heart and spirit ever bold,
Harriet Tubman, a tale to be oft told.*

## Early Life and The Chains That Bound

Fair Harriet Tubman, born into this realm of woe as Araminta Ross, did suffer the grievous yoke of bondage in Maryland's shadowed lands.  'Pon the cruel plantation, her tender years were steeped in servitude, a fate unjustly ordained. Though physical afflictions marked her youthful days, a spirit of unyielding resilience flickered within, a testament to the indomitable soul God had bestowed. 'Twas through clandestine whispers and the yearning for liberty that the seeds of her future daring were sown.

## The Conductor on the Underground Rail

When Providence smiled, and opportunity knocked, Mistress Tubman, as she came to be known, embraced her calling with valor. Escaping her shackles in 1849, she became a veritable "Conductor" upon the clandestine "Underground Railroad."  With fathomless courage, she traversed perilous paths, guiding brethren yearning for emancipation through shadowed forests and treacherous routes.  Dozens, nay, scores she led to sanctuary, risking her own freedom time and again, a veritable angel of deliverance.

## A Champion of Freedom's Flame

Her legend grew, a whisper on the wind, a symbol of defiance against tyranny.  Not content with mere escape, Mistress Tubman wielded her newfound liberty as a weapon. She returned repeatedly to the South, braving capture to liberate the enslaved. Her daring raids, fueled by unwavering faith and righteous anger, shook the very foundations of bondage. A spy for the Union cause during the War Between the States, she served with distinction,  leading raids and tending to the wounded, a multifaceted heroine.

## Legacy Etched in Freedom's Hall

Though mortal coil did claim her in 1913, Harriet Tubman's spirit endures, a pillar of fortitude and compassion. Her name resonates through the corridors of history, a testament to the triumph of the human spirit over oppression. She stands as a shining exemplar, reminding all that courage, faith, and unyielding love for fellow man can shatter even the darkest chains. Her path, paved with sacrifice and valor, forever illuminates the road towards true liberty.
