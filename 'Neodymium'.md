# Neodymium

*A hymn unto Neodymium, metal of azure hue,*
*Whose strength doth rival titans, and lustre pierceth through.*

## Description and Properties

Neodymium, a noble element borne of the Earth's fiery womb, presenteth itself in a visage of argent-gray, albeit tinged with subtle cerulean whispers. This metalloid, of the lanthanide kin, possesseth a density exceeding that of common lead, a testament to its inner fortitude.  Its allure lieth not merely in visual grace, but in magnetic prowess unmatched save by its brethren within the rare earth choir. A magnet forged with neodymium biteth with a force many times stronger than its ferrous counterparts, a boon to artisans and mages alike.
