## Taxonomy and Nomenclature

This prodigious creature, *Giganotosaurus carolinii*, doth belong unto the lineage of theropod dinosaurs, a race renowned for their carnivorous prowess.  Descended from ancestors of noble bearing, 'Giganto' as its cognomen implies,  signifieth "giant southern lizard,"  a moniker most fitting given its gargantuan stature and Argentinian provenance, where its petrified remains were first unearthed.
