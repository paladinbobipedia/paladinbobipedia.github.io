# Solid

*An Ode to Solidity, Everconstant and True*
Hark! A virtue most noble, 'Solid' doth stand, a pillar 'gainst Chaos' shifting sand. Unwavering in heart, and form unbent, a bastion 'gainst folly, heaven-sent. In steadfastness it finds its might, a beacon burning ever bright.

## Definition & Essence
"Solid," quoth we, signifies a state of unwavering integrity, both corporeal and metaphysical. 'Tis the antithesis of frailty,  a resistance to corruption, be it physical decay or moral subversion. A soul Solid as the adamantine, anchored in virtue, immune to the siren songs of vice.  In essence, it speaks to a grounded resilience, a monument to steadfast purpose.

## Manifestations in the Mundane
Witness 'Solid' in the stoic oak, weathering tempest's wrath; behold it in the mason's craft, where stones, though diverse, bind as one strong form.  A knight clad in gleaming armor, his loyalty a shield impenetrable, embodies this trait. Even in humbler deeds, a craftsman's unwavering skill, a scholar's steadfast pursuit of knowledge, these are echoes of 'Solidity's' gentle reign.

## The Paladin's Burden & Solidity

Our vows demand an unbreakable spirit, a bulwark against temptation and the encroaching darkness.
