# London Stock Exchange

*Hark, a hallowed ground where fortunes dance, a temple not of stone but of commerce's trance, the London Stock Exchange, a titan bold, its very name doth stories of wealth unfold.*

## Genesis and Grandeur

This venerable institution, sprung forth in yon year of Our Lord sixteen ninety-five, did first take root as a humble conventicle 'neath Royal Exchange's hallowed dome. A gathering of merchants, their coffers keen, to barter shares in nascent enterprises unseen. Thus, from whispered agreements, a mighty bourse evolved, a marketplace where capital doth revolve,  birthing corporations grand and small, its influence reaching far o'er land and wall.
