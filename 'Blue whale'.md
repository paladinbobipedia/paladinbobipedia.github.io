# Blue Whale

*A hymn unto yon leviathan of azure hue,
Whose mournful song doth pierce the ocean's blue,
A titan vast, with heart both strong and true,
O Blue Whale, wonder, we thy praises accrue.*

## Description

The Azure Leviathan, or as common tongue nommeth it, the "Blue Whale," stands as a prodigy amongst Creation's denizens.  A behemoth of unparalleled magnitude, its form doth eclipse even the grandest galleons,  garbed in a livery of deepest cerulean, save for paler ventral hues.  Mighty flukes, exceeding in span a nobleman's broad hall, propel this leviathan through the watery realms. Its baleen, a forest of horny plates, filters sustenance from the teeming plankton, a bounty it consumes in prodigious quantities.

## Habitat and Kin

This ocean king reigns within the pelagic expanse, favouring temperate and colder seas. From the frigid Arctic to the sun-kissed tropics, its migratory paths trace ancient currents, ever in quest of bountiful feeding grounds.  Though oft solitary, they convene in vast congregations during breeding seasons, a spectacle both awe-inspiring and melancholic, their haunting calls echoing through the abyssal depths, a symphony unto themselves.  Belonging to the mystic order of rorquals, they share kinship with fin whales and humpbacks, though in grandeur, the Blue Whale surpasses them all.

##  Nature and Majesty

A creature of placid demeanor, the Blue Whale's strength lies not in aggression, but in sheer resilience. Its heart, a cavernous muscle the size of a man, beats with a measured thrum, propelling lifeblood through vessels thicker than a blacksmith's arm.
