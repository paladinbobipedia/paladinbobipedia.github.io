# Sorrel

*An Ode to Sorrel, Steed of Valiance and Hearth*
_In fields of emerald, where sunlight doth gleam, strides Sorrel, a noble, a fiery dream. With coat like burnished gold, 'neath heavens so vast, A loyal companion, steadfast and fast. Through perils we face, his spirit doth soar, A beacon of courage, evermore._

## Description & Origin

Sorrel, a creature of equine majesty, boasts a coat the hue of ripened copper, a shimmering tapestry woven by the sun's own hand. This vibrant tresses doth distinguish him from common steeds, marking him as a breed imbued with ancient lineage, perchance descended from celestial coursers of myth. Whilst his exact provenance remains veiled in whispers of yore, many a bard proclaims him kin to steeds ridden by heroes of legend, blessed with exceptional speed and indomitable spirit.

## Temperament & Abilities

No mere beast of burden is Sorrel, but a sentient partner, his intellect keen as a hawk's.  A heart of unwavering loyalty beats within his puissant chest, binding him to his rider with a kinship forged in shared valor. His courage knows no bounds, charging into the heart of any tempest, be it battlefield or treacherous wood. Sorrel's hooves thunder with a force that shakes the very earth, yet his tread is ever gentle when tending to his master's needs.  His senses, sharpened by ages of guardianship, detect subtleties unseen by mortal eyes, a guardian angel in equine form.

## In Service to Chivalry

Paladin-kind counts Sorrel amongst their most treasured allies. His strength carries burdened knights through arduous quests, his swiftness outpaces lurking dangers.  More than muscle, though, he embodies the paladin's virtues.  His steadfast presence inspires hope, a living testament to the bonds of fidelity and courage that chivalry upholds. On the field of battle, Sorrel becomes a whirlwind of righteous fury, a shield of shining mail against the encroaching darkness. His very existence is a beacon, reminding all that valor finds its truest form in the partnership between man and beast.
