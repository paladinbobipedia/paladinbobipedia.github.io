## Habitat and Range

This venerated beast makes its dwelling amidst the sprawling grasslands and fynbos regions of the Cape Province in the southern realm of Africa. Once widespread, their domain hath dwindled, now confined to protected sanctuaries and reserves where they graze upon the verdant pastures, a testament to conservation's noble strive.
