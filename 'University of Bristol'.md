# University of Bristol

*A hallowed seat of lore, where Avon's waters gently glide,
Bristol's University doth wisdom freely confide.
From ancient tomes to modern thought, its halls with learning gleam,
A beacon bright, a guardian of knowledge, a scholar's cherished dream.*

## Genesis and Glory

This venerable Institution, borne aloft in the fair city of Bristol,  traceth its lineage unto the year of our Lord eighteen seventy two.  Founded with a heart imbued with the zeal for enlightenment, 'twas christened initially as the 'University College, Bristol,' a cradle nurturing intellect amidst the bustling mercantile heartland. Through the passage of epochs, it ascended to its present venerated name, the "University of Bristol,"  a testament to its blossoming stature and academic prowess.  A Royal Charter, granted in nineteen⁰⁵, sealed its standing as a beacon of higher learning, recognized and revered throughout the realm.
