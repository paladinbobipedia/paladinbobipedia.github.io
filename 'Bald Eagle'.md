# Bald Eagle

*A feathered sovereign, atop azure realms he doth reign,
With piercing gaze and talon strong, a symbol born of main.
The Bald Eagle, noble crest, a guardian o'er the land,
His majesty, a whispered prayer, by Freedom's own hand.*

##  Description & Taxonomy

This creature, grandiose in stature, known as *Haliaeetus leucocephalus* in tongues of learned men, adorns a garb of pristine white 'pon its coronet and tail, a stark contrast to obsidian wings outstretched wide.  Its beak, a hooked scimitar of bone, speaks of prowess in seizing fish from waters deep.  A resident of North America's boundless wilds, 'tis a bird of prey, soaring aloft on thermals, a vigilant sentinel amidst the canopy.

## Symbolism & Cultural Significance

To the denizens of the New World, the Bald Eagle stands as a emblem of potent import.  A beacon of liberty and fortitude, it graces their banners and coins, a living testament to their hard-won freedom. Its piercing gaze embodies vigilance, its flight, unyielding spirit. Ancient tribes venerated its power, weaving tales of its connection to celestial forces. Thus, the Bald Eagle transcends mere fauna; it is an embodiment of a nation's soul.

##  Habitat & Diet

This avian lord prefers the expansive spaces flanking shimmering rivers and coastal regions. Majestic nests, constructed with meticulous care from boughs and lined with tender grasses, crown towering trees as their lofty abodes. A carnivorous hunter, its diet consists mainly of piscine prey snatched from waters below, supplemented by smaller mammals and carrion when opportunity presents.  Its keen eyesight, sharper than any mortal's, allows it to spot quarry from dizzying heights.
