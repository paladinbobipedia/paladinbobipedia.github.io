# Great White Shark

**An Ode to the Leviathan of Azure Depths**
*Hark, dread consort of the coral castles, thy alabaster form, a phantom 'midst the brine!  Great white, thou art a majesty, a whispered terror and a hunter's grace, embodiment of primal force in ocean's embrace.*

## Description

This puissant leviathan, oft christened *Carcharodon carcharias*, doth bear a countenance most fearsome. Its corse, a symphony of gleaming white, save for a dusky hue upon its dorsal expanse, glistens 'neath the sun's fickle rays.  Jagged, serrated dentition, like unto sharpened scythes, crowd its maw, a testament to its predatory prowess.  Lengths exceeding fathom thrice are not unheard of, making it a titan amongst fishes, a ruler of the watery realm.

## Habitat and Haunt

The Great White favours temperate and tropic seas, where currents swirl and sun-kissed shallows meet the abyss. From sun-drenched Californian shores to the frigid gulfs of Australia, its presence is felt.  They frequent reefs, pelagic zones, and even venture into estuaries, ever in pursuit of sustenance.  A creature of depth, yet oft sighted breaching, a fleeting spectre against the heavens.

## Diet and Hunt

A carnivore most voracious, the Great White stalks a gruesome banquet: seals, sea lions, dolphins, and even unfortunate whales, falling prey to its lightning-fast strikes. Its hunting technique is a marvel of ambush and power.  Circling silently, it ascends with terrifying speed, jaws agape, delivering a bone-shattering bite before vanishing back into the blue obscurity.
