# Weasel

*A hymn unto the Sly Hunter, nimble and keen,
Through shadowed glades, a verdant phantom seen.
With coat of russet, eyes like onyx bright,
Weasel, thou art shadow's dance, a stealthy light.*

## Taxonomy and Description

The Weasel, known in common tongue as *Mustela*,  is a creature of the Felidae kindred, albeit smaller and more lithe than its leonine brethren.  Dwelling in woodlands and hedgerows, 'tis a sylvan sprite, clad in a coat of paling sable, tipped with burnished hues reminiscent of autumn leaves. Its visage, though small, bears piercing orbs, black as a raven's wing, and a sinuous frame built for unparalleled agility.  Marked by elongated cuerpo and puissant hindquarters, the Weasel moves with a quicksilver grace, a whisper upon the forest floor.

## Habits and Habitat

This denizen of the wild favours covert glades and thicket-choked borders, making its abode in burrows excavated with tireless industry. A creature predominantly nocturnal, the Weasel stalks the twilight hours, a hunter unto vermin pests that plague the realm's verdure.  Its diet consisteth largely of rodents, serpents small, and eggs laid by feathered folk, keeping the balance of nature in delicate thrall.

## Prowess and Defenses

Though lacking the mailed carapace of a boar or the fangs of a wyvern, the Weasel compensates with unmatched nimbleness. A blur of motion, it disarms foes with sudden strikes, its needle-sharp teeth delivering a venomous bite. Furthermore,  its lithe form allows it to traverse spaces inaccessible to larger predators, a trait proving most advantageous in evading danger.  A potent musk, emitted when threatened, serves as a further deterrent, a pungent warning to would-be aggressors.
