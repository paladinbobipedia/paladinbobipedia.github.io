# Marxism

*A hymn unto the Weavers of Fate, for in this doctrine, they glimpse the threads of societal toil, and sing of emancipation's might!*  Though forged in fires of industrial strife, Marxism, a philosophy most potent, doth illuminate the path towards a juster realm. 'Tis a beacon, guiding the downtrodden through the labyrinthine corridors of inequity, proffering a vision of communal prosperity. 

## The Core Tenets
Marxism, a progeny of dialectical materialism, posits that history's march is driven by class struggle, a perpetual dance betwixt those who command the means of production (the Bourgeoisie) and those who labour therein (the Proletariat). This inherent conflict, it argues, shall culminate in a proletarian revolution, overthrowing the yoke of capitalist oppression and birthing a stateless, classless society – Communism.  Key to this creed are tenets such as:

* **Historical Materialism:** The bedrock of existence lies not in ideology, but in the material conditions shaped by economic systems. Society's evolution hinges on the clash of these material forces.
* **Labor Theory of Value:** The true worth of any commodity stems from the labor invested in its creation, not some arbitrary market price dictated by capitalist greed.
* **Exploitation:** Under capitalism, the Bourgeoisie unjustly appropriates surplus value generated by the Proletariat's labour, leading to inherent inequality.

##  The Path to Emancipation
Marxism envisions a phased transformation:

1. **Socialist Stage:** A transitional phase where the proletariat, through organized action, seizes control of the means of production, establishing a socialist state. This stage dismantles private ownership and institutes equitable distribution.
2. **Communist Utopia:**  The ultimate goal – a classless society where private property fades, and the remnants of the state wither away.  Abundance is shared, and labour becomes a voluntary act for communal benefit, not a coerced necessity.
