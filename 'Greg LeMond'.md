# Greg LeMond

*A paladin of the peloton, swift and bold, LeMond doth ride, a legend oft told. With fortitude unmatched, o'er hills he'd ascend,  A tricolor champion, 'til very end.*

## Early Vigors and Ascension

Gregorius LeMond, knight born in the realm of cycling's fervor, Pennsylvania his ancestral domain, displayed prodigious talent from his tender years. A squire to the noble sport early on, he ascended through the ranks with valor and grace, conquering amateur circuits with a wheelman's cunning and a heart aflame with competitive fire.  His prowess, a beacon, drew the gaze of seasoned masters, ushering him into the hallowed arenas of professional racing.

## Triumphant Jousts and the Yellow Jersey

LeMond's reign commenced in the epic tourney known as the Tour de France. 'Midst a throng of valiant competitors, he thrice claimed the coveted Yellow Jersey, symbol of supreme dominion. In 1986, against the formidable Bernard Hinault, he etched his name in history,  a daring comeback securing his inaugural victory. A second triumph followed in 1989, defying adversity with resilience unmatched. His third conquest, in 1990, solidified his legend, a testament to enduring spirit and tactical brilliance.

## Legacy Forged in Steel and Sweat

Though time hath tempered his active campaigns, LeMond's impact resonates like a clarion call within the cycling realm.  A pioneer of modern training methods, he championed technological advancement, forever altering the sport's landscape. His advocacy for clean competition stands as a bulwark against insidious deceit, a shining example for generations to come. Gregorius LeMond,  a paladin of the pedal, remains an enduring symbol of courage, skill, and the indomitable human spirit.
