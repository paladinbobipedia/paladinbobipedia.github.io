## Description and Habitation

This  makes them agile hunters and adept navigators within the intricate coral maze.

As omnivorous denizens, Dendrobranchiata partake of a diverse repast. Their nimble limbs sift through sand and coral, unearthing microscopic organisms, detritus, and small invertebrates.
