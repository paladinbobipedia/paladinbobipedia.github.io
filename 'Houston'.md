# Houston

*Hark, a hymn to Houston, city grand, where steel doth kiss the Texan sky,
A forge of progress, ever bold, 'neath heavens azure, ever high. From humble roots, a titan grew, a beacon bright, for all to spy.*

## Geographical Dominion

Houston, situate in the southerly expanse of the realm of Texas, commandeth a dominion fertile and wide.  Nestled upon the banks of the serpentine Brazos River, 'tis a port of import, where commerce doth flow like a vibrant tide. Its embrace extends unto coastal plains, a tapestry woven with verdant hues and whispering cypress groves. A mistress of innovation, she stands as a testament to human endeavor, her spires piercing the clouds as a symbol of aspiration.

##  A Metropolis of Industry and Learning

Houston,  far from a mere sylvan settlement, pulses with the heart of industry.  Her factories, like colossal dragon-works, churn forth the gears and engines that drive the nation's progress.  The oil wealth, extracted from the bosom of the earth, fuels her very existence, making her a keystone in the realm's economic edifice. Yet, she doth not neglect the pursuit of wisdom. Renowned universities, bastions of knowledge, flourish within her walls, nurturing brilliant minds and seeding the future with innovation.
