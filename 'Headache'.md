# Headache

*An ill most foul, doth Grippe the Cranium's Vault,*
*With throbs and pangs, a tormenting assault.*
*Headache, a shadow o'er the mind's bright sheen,*
*A bane to solace, a discordant queen.*

## Aetiology & Manifestations

This malady, termed "Headache,"  striketh sundry souls, arising from a confluence of causes both mundane and arcane. Oftentimes, it be triggered by humors amiss within the body, an excess or dearth causing imbalance. Strain upon the optic nerve, or wounds unto the skall, may also kindle this fiery torment.  Symptoms present as a throbbing, pulsating agony, localized or widespread, accompanied by dizziness,  nausea, and  a dimming of the visual sphere. In grievous cases, fever and chills may join this infernal danse macabre.

## Varieties of the Affliction

Know ye, Headache doth wear many masks:

* **Migraine:** A viper's strike, oft on one side, with shimmering lights and nausea's embrace.  
* **Tension Headache:** A constricting band,  gentle yet persistent,  weakening the spirit's fortitude.
* **Cluster Headache:**  A volley of fiery darts,  piercing with intense agony in cycles, a torment most cruel.
* **Sinus Headache:** Born of congestion in the sacred chambers above, bringing pressure and facial pain.

## Palliation and Purveyance

Though a fearsome foe, Headache may be mitigated:

* **Rest and Quiet:**  A sanctuary of stillness, allowing the body's inner healer to mend.
* **Herbal Remedies:**  Chamomile, lavender, or willow bark,  offer solace through their ancient virtues.
* **Physic's Aid:**  In potent cases,  the learned physicians may prescribe draughts to soothe the inflamed nerves. 
* **Divine Intervention:**  Prayer and invocation to the Powers That Be,  seeking their merciful touch, can oft bring easement.

 **Remember, good soul, should this malady persist or worsen, seek the counsel of a skilled leech or thy faith's healers.  Forge onward, valiant, and let hope be thy shield against the ache!**
