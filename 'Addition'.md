## The Essence of Union

Addition, forsooth, is the sacred act of uniting discrete quantities into a singular, augmented whole.  Like unto merging righteous knights under a banner,  'tis the joining of numbers, be they small as pebbles or vast as celestial bodies, to forge a new magnitude. This union giveth birth to a resultant value, larger than its constituent parts, a testament to the power found in collective strength.

##  The Symbology of the Plus

Mark well, the symbol "+", a radiant cross signifying this holy merger.  It bespeaks not mere concatenation, but a transformative blending. As a Paladin's oath binds him to valor, so doth "+" bind numbers in their sacred pact, creating something novel and amplified.
