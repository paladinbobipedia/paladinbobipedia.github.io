# Employment

*Hark, noble seeker of knowledge, lend thine ear to a discourse on Employment, that vital pillar upholding the very fabric of our societal order.  A righteous calling, 'tis a dance betwixt mortal toil and divine purpose, weaving prosperity's tapestry through honest endeavour. Like a stalwart knight shielding his realm, doth worthy employment defend against idleness's blight, fostering virtue and uplifting the common good.*

## Definitions and Virtues

Employment, in its purest form, denoteth the honourable exertion of one's skills and labour within the framework of a structured endeavor, be it in fields of craftsmanship, commerce, or service. 'Tis a sacred duty, binding the individual to a noble purpose,  and fostering self-reliance whilst contributing to the commonwealth's flourishing. Such honest toil instills discipline, cultivates diligence, and breathes life into the very arteries of our kingdom.

##  Varieties of Calling

### The Artisan's Craft

From the blacksmith shaping steel with fiery grace, to the weaver threading silken tales, the Artisan's hand embodies creation's magic. Their labours, born of skill and dedication, provide sustenance and beauty, enriching lives with tangible expressions of human ingenuity. 

###  The Merchant's Exchange

The Merchant, a vital conduit of trade,  facilitates the flow of goods and sustenance across lands. Their acumen in commerce, a righteous stewardship, ensures provisions reach far corners, underpinning the prosperity of all.  Honesty and fairness in their dealings are paramount, for they weave the threads of economic vitality.

### The Servitor's Duty

From tending to the realm's infrastructure to ministering to its people's needs, the Servitor's role is one of quiet yet indispensable valor.  They are the backbone, the unseen heroes upholding the very fabric of our society through diligence and compassion. Their service, a testament to humility and loyalty, deserves the highest esteem.

##  The Paladin's Perspective on Worthy Employment

A Paladin, sworn to virtue and justice, views employment not merely as a means to coin, but as a sacred vocation.  Each honest labour, executed with integrity, aligns with the divine order.  Seek ye not merely a task, but a calling that resonates with thine inner righteousness, contributing to the greater good. Thus, through toil infused with purpose, doth a person truly flourish, mirroring the radiant light of our Creator.
