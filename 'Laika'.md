## Origins and Calling

Laika, a creature of humble canine birth, hailed from the earthly realms, a Shepherd dog of modest stir. Yet, chosen by fate's unseen hand, she was entrusted with a destiny grand. The progenitors of Sputnik, those masters of mechanized might, selected her for a voyage most rare, a pioneering flight into the starlit glare. 'Tis said her lineage, though common seeming, held within a valiant heart, ever dreaming.  A testament to courage, she volunteered, nay, *embraced* this journey unreserved.
