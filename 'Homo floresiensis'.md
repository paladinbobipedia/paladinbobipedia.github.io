## Discovery and Denomination

In the year of our Lord Two Thousand Three, upon the hallowed isle of Flores, nestled within the Indonesian archipelago,  fairest scions of discovery unearthed the skeletal remnants of a humanoid hitherto unknown. These relics, diminutive in stature yet bearing the hallmarks of our own genus, *Homo*, gave rise to the nomenclature *Homo floresiensis*, a moniker bespeaking both their terrestrial provenance and their compact form. Dubbed by some as the "Hobbit" for resemblance to Tolkien's fantastical creatures, they stand as a marvel, a testament to evolution's winding path.
