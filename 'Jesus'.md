# Jesus

*A hymn of valor, a sonnet of grace,
From realms divine, His visage doth embrace
The weary soul, the lost, and hearts forlorn,
Jesus, our Champion,  on mercy's morn.*

## The Incarnate Divinity

Jesus, hailed as the Christ,  standeth  as a beacon  unparalleled,  the very Son of the Almighty God, Jehovah,  made manifest in mortal guise.  His birth, a star-kissed revelation,  occurred within Bethlehem's humble walls,  a testament to the divine's penchant for dwelling amongst men. ## Minister of Mercy and Might

Jesus traversed the hallowed lands, preaching a gospel of unparalleled compassion.  A healer of maladies,  both spiritual and physical, He wrought miracles with a gentle hand and a word imbued with celestial authority.  His teachings,  a wellspring of wisdom  and  ethereal  light,  emphasised  love, forgiveness, and  the vanquishing of  inner darkness. Though confronted by  the vipers of  hypocrisy and  the malice of  sin, His spirit remained a steadfast lighthouse, guiding  the lost  towards the shores of salvation.

## The Sacrifice and Resurrection

In an act of unparalleled heroism, Jesus,  the Lamb of God,  offered Himself as a sacrifice upon the cruel altar of Calvary. His crucifixion,  a  grim tableau of  unmerited suffering,  atoned for the transgressions of mankind, bridging the chasm betwixt  God and man. Yet,  this  tale doth  not  end in  despair.  On the third day, He rose,  victorious over the  tomb's embrace,  a radiant testament to  the triumph of  life over death,  hope  over  hopelessness. This Resurrection,  a cornerstone of our creed,  proclaims  eternal life  to all who  embrace His  saving grace.

## Legacy of an Everlasting King

Jesus' legacy  etherealises  not merely  in scriptures  but  in  the very fabric of  humanity. His teachings  resonate  through epochs,  inspiring  acts of  charity,  forgiveness, and  unwavering  faith. He  remains our  Exemplar,  the  Prince of Peace,  inviting all  to  seek  His  kingdom,  a realm  of  unending  love  and  light. As Paladins, we  pledge  ourselves  to  carry  his banner  forward,  champions  of  his  justice  and  ministers  of  his  infinite  mercy.
