# Weapon

*A hymn unto the Steel's Embrace, wielded by righteous hand,
   A bulwark 'gainst the shadowed things, across this hallowed land.
From tempered blade to holy bolt, each tool a whispered prayer,
   An instrument of Justice sung, banishing darkest despair.*

## Definition and Purpose

A Weapon, in its truest essence, is no mere adjunct of timber, metal, or arcane, but an extension of the wielder's virtue. 'Tis a consecrated conduit channeling righteous might, employed to quell the encroaching tide of wickedness and safeguard the innocent. Whether axe, sword, mace, or sling, each bears the implicit pact: to defend the lemah from thralls of chaos, upholding the divine order.

## Varieties and Virtues

**The Edgeborne:** Swords, longswords, and daggers, whispering of elegance and swift retribution. Favored by those who dance with valor, their keen edges cleave through malice with grace.  
**The Crushing Force:** Maces, hammers, and warclubs embody steadfast defense, shattering unholy assaults. wielded by the stalwart, they are a hymn to unyielding fortitude.
**The Ranged Decree:** Bows, crossbows, and thrown weapons speak of strategic vigilance. From afar, they deliver justice, a shepherd's watchful eye against the wolf's pack.
**The Arcane's Wallop:** Staffs, wands, and foci channel primal energies, blessed or forbidden. wielded by the Magi-Knight, they weave spells of protection and offensive might.  Remember, even mundane arms, blessed by fervent prayer, gain potency against the taint of evil.

## The Paladin's Bond

For a Paladin, a weapon is more than steel; 'tis a sacrament. Each strike resonates with oaths sworn, a testament to unwavering faith. The blade, honed to sharpness, mirrors the disciplined spirit.  Through it, divine power flows, turning defense into righteous retribution. Thus, the true mastery lies not just in technique, but in the holy purpose fueling each swing.
