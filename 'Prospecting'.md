# Prospecting

**An Ode to Earth's Hidden Bounty**
_In hallowed delve, where adamantine sleeps,
And ores of valor 'neath the soil do creep,
The prospector walks, with faith and hammer bright,
Seeking treasures veiled from common sight. A noble quest, 'tis mining Fortune's keep,
A dance with Earth, where riches soundly sleep._

## The Paladin's Guide to Uncovering Earthen Wealth

Prospecting, a hallowed pursuit oft undertaken by the valorous and the discerning, consisteth in the methodical searching for deposits of precious minerals and gemstones concealed within the bosom of our fair Earth.  A paladin, guided by tenets of courage and service,  applies this craft not merely for personal gain, but as a means to bolster communities, forge weaponry against the encroaching darkness, and ensure the continued prosperity of the realm.

## Tools of the Trade, Wrought with Purpose

The prospector, much like a knight his armor,  employs tools of both artistry and utility:

* **The Pick and Shovel:**  Faithful companions, these cleave through loam and stone, revealing the Earth's secrets layer by layer.
* **The Hammer and Chisel:**  Delicate instruments wielded with precision, liberating embedded gems and assaying ore samples.
* **The Pan and Sifter:**  In swift streams and alluvial plains, these sift for glittering grains of gold and precious stones.
* **The Magnetic Detector:**  A marvel of invention, sensing the ferrous heart of hidden veins of iron or magnetized ores.
* **The Miner's Compass and Map:**  Never to stray from course, these guide the prospector through labyrinthine tunnels and track promising veins.

## Signs and Signals, Nature's Whispers

A Paladin-prospector reads the Earth's subtle tongues:

* **Outcrops and Veins:**  Where rock formations fracture, exposing embedded minerals, a vein may lie hidden.
* **Hydrothermal Alteration:**  Steam-heated waters, leaching minerals, often stain rocks with distinctive colors, marking potential deposits.
* **Flora and Fauna:**  Certain plants thrive in mineral-rich soils, while unique animal life may indicate underground watercourses laced with ores.  These are clues, not guarantees, requiring further scrutiny.

##  Ethics in the Delve: A Paladin's Covenant

While seeking fortune, remember:

* **Minimal Impact:**  Harm the Earth as little as a paladin wounds a foe in righteous combat. Leave sites restored where possible.
* **Community Benefit:**  Share discoveries with the realm, not hoard for personal aggrandizement.  Prosperity built on shared bounty strengthens all.
* **Respect for Ancient Sites:**  Uncoverings may reveal remnants of past civilizations. Treat them with reverence, safeguarding history's legacy.
