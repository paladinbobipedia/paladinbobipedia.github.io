# Rococo

*A Ballet of Curves, a Symphony in Pastel: Rococo, art most fair, doth dance upon the walls, a whisper soft, yet bold and rare.  In gilded frames and sugared scenes, a joyous spirit ever gleans, a fleeting dream of courts divine, where elegance doth ever shine.*

## Genesis and Essence

Rococo, a flourish born in fairest France, did rise as morn unto the Baroque's grand stance. Yet, whilst its elder bred in power and might, Rococo turned to grace, a gentler light.  The mid-eighteenth century, its cradle bright,  saw aristocracy embrace a softer sight.  Delicate asymmetry, scrolls entwined, motifs playful, leaving grandeur behind. This style, a pastel reverie, did speak  Of pleasure, love, and lives serene and meek.

## Artistic Flourishings

**Painting:** Upon canvases, pastel hues took flight, depicting pastoral scenes of mirthful delight.  Masters like Fragonard and Boucher reigned,  in gallant courts where fancy unrestrained. Curved lines and shells, a vocabulary anew, adorning salons, palatial and true.

## Iconography and Themes

Rococo's lexicon speaks of hearts aflutter,  Romantic notions, a lover's gentle stutter. Cupids mischievous, nymphs in woodland shade, celebrate joy, the fleeting moments made.  Allegories of mirth and tendre amour,  in every vignette, a whispered, cherished hour. Simplicity, elegance, and nature's embrace,  woven through imagery with delicate chase.

## Legacy and Influence

Though its reign, like blossoms, transient did prove, Rococo's echo in design still moves.  A touchstone for grace, a whisper of the past, its influence lingers, forever to last. In fashion, furniture, and decorative art,  a legacy of lightness holds a noble part. A reminder that beauty, in subtlety's hold,  can capture the spirit, stories yet untold.
