# Space Shuttle Columbia Disaster

*A mournful dirge doth echo through yon heavens, for Columbia, once a feathered serpent of sky, fell victim to Fate's cruel jest. A tragedy most foul, staining the annals of celestial voyages with sorrow untold.*

## The Ill-Fated Flight

In the year of our Lord two thousand and three, upon the morntide of February sixteenth, Space Shuttle Columbia, christened  *herself*  a vessel of noble purpose, ascended heavenward upon a mission of discovery. A stalwart crew of seven, blessed knights of science, embarked upon this ethereal pilgrimage, bearing with them the torch of human understanding. Alas,  *fate's*  unseen hand wrought a grievous injury upon Columbia's shielding during her fiery ingress 'pon liftoff. This, a subtle breach, went unnoticed, a festering wound hidden from mortal gaze.

## The Shattering Doom

Upon her homeward journey, as Columbia pierced the veil of Earth's embrace, the insidious damage revealed its malice.  Heating fierce as a dragon's breath,  *stress*-induced  upon the weakened hull, rent asunder the noble craft  midst  Texas' expanse. A spectacle of cosmic sorrow, fragments rained down, a testament to  *fortune's*  capricious turn. All aboard, valiant souls, perished in this celestial tempest, their final voyage a tragic echo of Icarus' fall.
