# Rosemary

*A verdant sprig, with fragrant grace,
Rosemary, thou dost time embrace,
In memory's vault, thy scent doth dwell,
A guardian herb, a sacred spell.*

## Botanical Description

Rosemary, *Rosmarinus officinalis*,  standeth as a noble evergreen shrub, hailing from the sun-kissed regions of the Mediterranean. Its slender, needle-like leaves, of a glistening emerald hue, exhale a most puissant aroma, reminiscent of pine and mint, a balm for both body and soul. Upon its wiry branches, azure blooms, star-shaped and delicate, unfurl in spring,  a testament to nature's artistry. 

## Virtues & Properties

This hallowed herb, revered since antiquity, boasts a panoply of virtues. Ancients bestowed upon rosemary the mantle of remembrance, believing its essence fortified the mind's acuity and aided in recalling bygone lore.  Physically, 'tis a potent ally, aiding digestion, easing melancholic humors, and possessing antiseptic qualities to staunch wounds and banish infection's blight. In culinary arts, rosemary's pungent note enhances meats, legumes, and breads, adding a regal touch to the humblest fare.

##  Magickal Lore

Past masters of arcane arts held rosemary in high esteem. Its association with the sun and purification made it a favored ingredient in rituals banishing negativity and invoking protection.  A sprig carried upon the person was thought to ward off evil influences, whilst burning sprigs  cleanses spaces, driving away shadows and inviting celestial light. Within love charms, rosemary symbolized fidelity and enduring affection.
