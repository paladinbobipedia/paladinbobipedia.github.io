# Animal

*A hallowed hymn to yon creatures, great and small, Whose spirits roam, answering Nature's call. From feathered finch to serpent sly, They weave the tapestry 'neath Heaven's eye, A symphony of life, both fierce and mild, In God's grand design, forever wild.*

## Definition & Essence

"Animal," prithee, doth denote a realm of beings, diverse in form and function, yet united by a primal essence. Unlike mankind, crafted in the Divine Image, animals dwell in a realm more instinctual, governed by the ancient laws of nature. They are embodiments of raw vitality, expressions of Creation's boundless artistry, inhabiting every niche from sun-drenched glades to caverns deep. 

##  Diversity & Classification

The bestiary of this Earth is vast and wondrous, a veritable chronicle of evolution's hand. We categorize these creatures into kindreds, as per their shared traits: the leonine predators, the equine grazers, the avian soarers, the squamous denizens of waters dark, and countless others. Each, a testament to the Creator's infinite ingenuity, tailored to its ecological role.  Though tongues may differ in naming, the kinship 'twixt them all is plain to a discerning eye.

##  Relationship to Mankind

Man, though blessed with reason and faith, stands interwoven with this animal tapestry. We share this terrestrial realm, reliant upon many creatures for sustenance, companionship, and even tutelage in the ways of wildness. Yet, a sacred duty binds us: to treat all life with reverence, respecting their place within the divine order.  Exploitation and cruelty are an affront, a perversion of the harmony ordained.
