# Arctic Tern

*Hark! A feathered wanderer, betwixt icy realm and sun-kissed shore, the Arctic Tern doth grace our skies, a paragon of steadfast journey and resilience bold. Its wings, like argent arrows, cleave the heavens,  a testament to nature's artistry, ever southward bound when winter's breath doth chill, and northward soaring when spring's rebirth unfurls. A symbol of unwavering spirit, it flies!*

## Habitation and Range
This noble avian, christened *Sterna paradisaea* by learned scribes, maketh its dwelling amidst the frigid expanses of the Arctic Circle in boreal summers.  A creature of prodigious wanderlust, 'tis renowned for the grandest migration amongst all winged kind.  From frozen tundras, it wings its way  southward, traversing oceans vast, unto the Antarctic's icy fringe, a circuit unmatched in avian annals. With spring's return, the epic journey reverses, retracing these celestial pathways to its northern breeding grounds. Thus, throughout the year, this tireless traveler graces both poles, a marvel of nature's design.

## Physiognomy and Attributes
The Arctic Tern presents a visage of elegant simplicity. Its plumage, predominantly white save for a slate-grey cap upon its head, mirrors the stark beauty of its habitat.  A black necklace adorns its throat, accentuating a keen, intelligent gaze. Wings, long and pointed, are built for tireless flight, enabling it to soar effortlessly on currents unseen by mortal eye. Its slender beak, sharp as a honed blade, is ideally suited for seizing small fishes, its primary sustenance, from the ocean's embrace.

## Diet and Foraging
A staunch carnivore, the Arctic Tern subsists primarily upon a diet of silver-scaled fishes gleaned from the sunlit shallows. With  astute eyesight and aerial prowess, it dives with astonishing accuracy, snatching unsuspecting prey in its formidable beak. ## Reproductive Prowess
Upon reaching its northern breeding grounds, the Arctic Tern engages in a courtship ritual as vibrant as the aurora borealis.  Upon earthen nests, meticulously crafted amidst grassy knolls or rocky outcrops, a single clutch of three to five speckled eggs is laid. Both parents, embodiments of dedicated kinship, share the weighty task of incubation and chick rearing.  Their young, fledglings swift of wing, embark upon their own epic journeys mirroring those of their progenitors, a testament to the enduring cycle of life woven into the fabric of this extraordinary species.
