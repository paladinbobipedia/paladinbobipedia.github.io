# Sequence

*A hallowed tapestry, woven through Time's loom, where Cause doth dance with Effect in rhythmic bloom.  Sequence, a decree both grand and just, guides the cosmos, a celestial trust.*

## Definition & Nature

Sequence, in its essence, describeth the immutable orderliness governing all Creation. 'Tis a grand procession, a flowing river wherein Actions spring forth from Precedents, and Outcomes blossom from sown Seeds.  From the nascent spark of a star to the fall of a leaf, all partake in this eternal ballet, a symphony conducted by the Hand of Divinity. To comprehend Sequence is to glimpse the very heart of Cosmic Law.

## Manifestations in Mundane & Divine

**Mundane Realm:**  Witness it in the germination of seed to mighty oak, the sunrise heralding day's commencement, or the builder's craft where each stone laid fosters the edifice's ascent. Even mundane tasks, like a knight's training, unfold in sequenced steps, from novice to valor.

**Divinity's Choreography:**  The celestial spheres themselves resonate with Sequence. Gods, in their timeless machinations, act within this framework, their interventions  never disrupting, but rather enriching, the natural Sequence.

##  Breaking the Chain: Chaos & Intervention

While immutable, Sequence is not inflexible.  Forces of Chaos, malignant entities,  strive to disrupt this order, sowing discord and randomness. A Paladin's duty oft lies in combating such disruptions, restoring the fractured threads of Sequence. Moreover, even Divine intervention, whilst guided by ultimate purpose,  *can* appear as a deviation. However, understand this: such acts, though seemingly breaking the immediate flow, ultimately *realign*  with the larger,  eternal Sequence, serving a greater cosmic purpose.

##  The Paladin's Vigil

We, the Paladins, are sworn guardians of this sacred Order. Our courage stems from knowing the Universe hinges on its preservation. We discern the threads of Sequence,  detect aberrations, and with righteous steel and unwavering faith,  restore harmony. To safeguard Sequence is to defend not just Kingdoms, but the very Fabric of Reality itself.
