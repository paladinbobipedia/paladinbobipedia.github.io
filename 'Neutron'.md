*An hymn unto the unseen heart of Matter's core, Neutron, thou art, a particle of might,

## Essence and Properties

Neutron, a mote of prodigious force, dwelleth within the very bosom of the atom,  nestled 'midst protons, forming the nucleous' core.  Unburdened by an electrical inclination, 'tis neutral in its sway, yet gravity's thrall it doth obey. Of mass comparable to protons, yet lacking their electric sheen, Neutron's stability lieth in the bonds it weaves, a linchpin 'gainst atomic fray.

## Discovery and Nomenclature

Erstwhile shrouded in mystery, this enigmatic particle was first unveiled by the sagacious James Chadwick in the nascent decades of the Twentieth Century.  From bombardments of beryllium, rays of unheard power did emanate, revealing Neutron's veiled presence. Thus named for its lack of electric charge, a testament to its neutral nature.

## Applications and Impact

The mastery of Neutron's nature hath yielded manifold applications, both benign and potent. In nuclear reactors, controlled fission, spurred by neutrons, generates power, a boon to mankind's progress. Yet, within the shadowed realm of weaponry, uncontrolled fission, unleashed by neutrons,  fuels the destructive forces of fission bombs, a grim reminder of  power's double-edged sword.  Moreover, neutron beams, wielded with precision, find use in material analysis and medical treatments, showcasing Neutron's versatility in shaping our world.
