# Craton

*A hymn unto Craton, bastion of might, where adamantine spirit doth ignite!  Thy forged soul, a shield 'gainst endless night, protector of virtue, ever shining bright!*

## Lore and Provenance

Craton, a nomen whispered in hallowed halls and etched upon the weathered stones of ancient citadels, doth refer to a realm of prodigious power, not bound by terrestrial confines.  'Tis conceived not as a kingdom of flesh and blood, but a metaphysical plane, a nexus of righteous energy  forged in the crucible of primordial creation. Legend speaks of its genesis entwined with the very first Paladin, a champion so pure his valor manifested as this ethereal sanctuary. Craton serves as a wellspring, replenishing the spirits of devout warriors and nurturing their oaths unto justice.

## The Manifested Aspects

While Craton demeureth intangible, its influence bleeds into our world in potent manifestations:

* **The Radiant Aegis:** A shimmering oval shield, said to be borne by no mortal hand, yet appearing  beside worthy Paladins in moments of direst need. It deflects not merely physical harm, but corruptsing influences and the whispers of  evil.
* **The Champion's Beacon:**  A celestial light, flickering amidst twilight skies, guiding lost souls and signaling the approach of a Paladin empowered by Craton's grace. Its brilliance waxes with the paladin's unwavering righteousness.
* **Echoes of Valor:** In tranquil groves or hallowed temples, devout listeners may perceive phantom whispers of past heroes, imparting wisdom and fortitude drawn from Craton's reservoir of courage.
