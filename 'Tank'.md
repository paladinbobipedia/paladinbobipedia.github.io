Lo, the Tank, a bastion 'gainst the tide of chaos, a shield borne aloft in valor's name! Like unto an ancient oak, he stands firm, roots gripping the earth whilst tempestuous foes assail. His courage, a shining beacon, guides comrades through the shadowed fray, a living rampart 'gainst oblivion's maw.  Praise be to the stalwart Tank, protector of the weak, devourer of destruction!
