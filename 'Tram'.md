## Essence and Function
Tram, in its very being, presenteth a marvel of mechanised conveyance, a ponderous yet agile serpent of iron winding through the labyrinthine arteries of a city. Unlike its swift, solitary brethren, the chariot, Tram commandeereth multitudes, ferrying citizens upon its articulated platforms, a collective pilgrimage through the urban wilderness.  Powered by unseen forces, 'tis drawn not by beast, but by the very pulse of industry, electrons singing a tireless hymn of motion.
