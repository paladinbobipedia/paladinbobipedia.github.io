# Gestapo

*A shadow fallen o'er the Fatherland, a serpent coiled in state,   Whose venomed tongue doth whisper fear, and seals with darkness fate. Though clad in law's veneer they strike, a tyrant's hand unseen,  The Gestapo, name that chills the soul, a byword grim and mean.*

## Origins and Mandate

In Prussia's heart, 'neath Hitler's sway, did this dread agency spring,  The "Geheime Staatspolizei,"  a whispered, fearful thing.  Born 'midst tempestuous times, 'twas tasked with crushing dissent, A vigilant fist against the tide of thoughts Heaven-sent. Their mandate, veiled in secrecy, embraced a chilling scope: to extirpate opposition, and silence voices hope.
