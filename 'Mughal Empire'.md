## Imperial Expanse and Administration

Stretching from Kabul's rugged hold to Deccan's southern, spicy fold, the Mughal realm encompassed lands of fertile yield and desert sands.  A masterful web of governance they spun, with provinces well-ordered, each one.  Mansabdari, a rank and file, rewarded service, martial and guile.  Akbar's hukumat, a just decree, ensured peace flowed like a tranquil sea.  Cities like Agra, Fatehpur Sikri, monuments to artistry, did flourish brightly.

## Artistic and Cultural Flourish

A renaissance 'neath the Mughal sway, where arts and learning held their day.  Architecture, a soaring flight, Taj Mahal, a teardrop of light,  a timeless masterpiece, etched in stone,  testament to love, forever known. Miniature paintings, delicate and fine, captured life's essence, scene by scene. Persian influences, interwoven bright, with indigenous strands, a radiant sight. Literature blossomed, verses sung,  Kabir's hymns, where truths were flung.  A fusion rich, a cultural blend, the Mughal epoch, without end.

## Decline and Legacy

Though time's relentless tide did creep,  and internal strife did softly seep,  the Mughal grandeur, though dimmed in hue, left an enduring mark, forever true.  Empires fall, yet legacies remain,  in monuments, customs, and whispered refrain. The Mughal Empire, a star that set, kindled a fire, India won't forget. Its spirit lives in woven thread,  a testament to what once was bred -  a fusion of worlds, a noble stand,  a legend etched upon the land.
