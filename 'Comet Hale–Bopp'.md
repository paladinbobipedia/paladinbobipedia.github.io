# Comet Hale–Bopp

*A celestial harbinger, with tail aflame, doth Hale-Bopp grace our heavens, a wondrous name.  Across the cosmic vastness, its icy splendor gleams, a beacon of the divine, in ethereal dreams.*

## Discovery and Nomenclature

In the morn of yon year, Nineteen ninety-five, twain learned gazers, Hale and Bopp did strive, with watchful eyes upon the starlit sphere, to spy this wanderer, banished from its lair. Thus christened it was, a union of their ken, Comet Hale–Bopp, a celestial phenomeon to beholden.

## Orbital Eccentricities

This cosmic nomad, on a path most strange, doth trace an orbit, lengthy and derange. 'Round Sol, our sun, 'tis flung in circuits vast, exceeding thrice the orbit Neptune casteth. Such elongated dance, a hallmark grand, speaks of a genesis  far beyond our land,  in the icy recesses where celestial bodies sleep. 

## A Spectacle Unmatched

With naked eye beheld, a shimmering light, Comet Hale–Bopp pierced the darkest night. Its coma, a hazy halo, brilliantly did glow, whilst its tail, a silken ribbon, o'er heavens did flow. A celestial artist, painting trails of green and gold, a sight to stir the soul, stories yet untold.

## Composition and Nature

An icy wanderer, composed of frozen might, silicates, and dust, bathed in eternal night. Volatile compounds, like water and methane's trace, slumber within its heart, awaiting Sol's embrace. When warmed by proximity, these secrets then unfurl,  forming the coma's glow, a cosmic, transient world. 

## Legacy and Wonder

Comet Hale–Bopp, a guest of fleeting stay, etched itself in memory,  brightening our way. A testament to the cosmos' boundless grace, a reminder of the wonders time cannot erase.  Scholars pore o'er its passage, seeking cosmic lore,  while stargazers still marvel, and legends evermore shall soar.
