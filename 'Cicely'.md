# Cicely

*An Ode unto Cicely, Maiden of Mirth and Might*
_A beacon she doth shine, with heart aflame,
In trials dire, her spirit doth proclaim
A steadfast virtue, courage ever bold,
Cicely, a name in tales of old to be told._

## Origins and Lineage

Cicely, whilst her precise progenitors veil'd in mists of time, hails from a lineage whispered amongst the folk as touched by celestial grace. Many a bard doth sing of ancestors, knights valiant and healers renowned, imbuing young Cicely with a nascent aura of righteousness from her very cradle. Though humble her birthplace may seem, 'tis said the stars themselves conspired to align upon her advent, marking her for a destiny interwoven with fate's grand tapestry.

## The Paladin's Path

Guided by an inner light most radiant, Cicely forsook the sheltered ease of lineage for the hallowed path of knighthood, embracing the tenets of Paladinhood with fervent devotion. Her oath, sworn beneath a silver moon, binds her to vanquish iniquity and shield the innocent. This sacred vow finds expression not merely in martial prowess, though she wields blade and shield with admirable skill, but more so in a compassion that flows like a healing spring, tending to both corporeal and spiritual wounds. 

## Deeds of Valor and Mercy

Her exploits, chronicled in ballads and etched upon the hearts of the grateful, speak volumes. From routing bandit hordes with righteous fury to soothing the afflicted with gentle words and restorative touch, Cicely embodies the Paladin's ideal.  Tales abound of her rescuing villages from monstrous thralls, mediating disputes with unwavering fairness, and offering sanctuary to the downtrodden. Each act, a testament to her courage tempered by mercy, solidifies her renown as a shield against the encroaching darkness.
