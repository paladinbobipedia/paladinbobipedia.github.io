# Optical Fiber

*A hymn unto the slender strand, a vein of light's swift hand,

## Nature and Composition

Optical fiber, a conduit most wondrous, fashioned from materials crystalline and pure.  Its very core, a filament of glass or plastic, crafted with meticulous care to exhibit a refractive index surpassing that of its outer cladding. This cladding, a sheath of subtly different composition, doth gently confine the light's embrace, guiding its passage along the fiber's length with ethereal fidelity. By this principle of total internal reflection, photons, the very quanta of light, dance and propagate, traversing vast distances unimpeded.

## Virtues and Applications

Optical fiber, a boon to mankind, boasts virtues manifold: unparalleled bandwidth, transmitting data at speeds exceeding the swiftness of sound. Immunity to electromagnetic interference renders it steadfast in tempestuous networks. Furthermore, its slender form allows for dense cabling, a boon for urban sprawls and intricate infrastructures. This marvel finds its use in myriad domains: from the warp-speed internet threading our connected world to telephony's ethereal whispers,  from  high-definition vision traversing continents to the delicate sensing of medical instruments peering within the body's sanctum. Truly, optical fiber is a linchpin of our modern age, a conduit of knowledge and connection.
