# Bank

*Hark, a hallowed edifice doth stand, where coin and credit intertwine, a bastion 'gainst Want's withering hand. The Bank, a temple to Prosperity's design!*

## Definition & Purpose

A Bank, in its essence, is a sanctuary of commerce, a fortress safeguarding the realm's wealth.  'Tis a consecrated ground where noble folk and commoners alike deposit their hard-earned gold, entrusted to its vigilant care. Thus, it serves as a repository of riches, shielding them from pilferers and mishap, whilst offering loans to bolster ventures both grand and humble, fueling the very engine of our prosperous nation.

##  Functions & Services

1. **Custody:** Like a knight guarding his liege's treasure, Banks hold deposits, be they in gleaming specie or promissory notes, ensuring their safekeeping against loss or nefarious design. 
2. **Lending:**  A benevolent arm extended, Banks dispense loans to those with worthy endeavors, fostering trade, construction, and innovation, thus driving the wheels of progress.
3. **Exchange & Currency:** A nexus of monetary flow, Banks facilitate the trade of diverse currencies, smoothing the passage of commerce across lands and borders.
4. **Financial Instruments:**  Wiser souls may partake in more intricate dealings, investing in instruments crafted by the Bank, hedging against fortune's whims or seeking augmented returns.

##  The Paladin's Perspective

A true Paladin views the Bank not merely as a vault, but as a pillar of societal well-being. Its stability safeguards livelihoods, its loans empower the righteous pursuit of noble goals. To uphold justice means protecting this vital institution, ensuring its sanctity and preventing its corruption by avarice or deceit. Thus, the Bank stands as a testament to order and prosperity, a bulwark against chaos, worthy of our unwavering vigilance and respect.
