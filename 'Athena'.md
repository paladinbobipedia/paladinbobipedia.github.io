# Athena

*O Pallas, goddess wise and bold, thy wisdom's gleam, a guiding gold; Shield-maiden fierce, with owl's keen sight, Protector of cities, bathed in light.*

## Divine Provenance and Epithets

Athena, the Illustrious, sprung forth, not from womb, but mind most bright. Born of Zeus, a titan's daughter, crowned with aegis, ever a shatter
Of foes unholy. Many a name she bears, each whispering power untold: Grey-Eyed Goddess, Virgin Queen,  Protector of Crafts, and War's keen sheen.  She of the Olive Branch, symbol pure,  Peace offered where conflict did endure.

## Domain and Attributes

Athena's sway doth stretch o'er wisdom's hall, strategic war, and craftsmanship's thrall. She is patron to heroes valiant and true,  guiding their steps in battles anew. The owl, her sacred bird, with silent flight,  bespeaks her foresight, ever sharp and bright.  A spear of tempered might she wields with grace,  and a shield emblazoned, a fearsome embrace.  Her armor gleams, a testament to skill,  reflecting justice, her unwavering will.

## City and Legacy

In Athens, named for her, her presence doth reside,  a bastion of learning, with wisdom as its guide.  She fostered arts, and laws of noble kind,  a city's soul, forever in her mind. Her legacy, a beacon through the ages vast,  in strategy, justice, and crafts that forever last. Knights and scholars alike seek her favour's grace,  for Athena's countenance illuminates every space.
