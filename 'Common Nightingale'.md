# Common Nightingale

*A hymn to yon feathered minstrel, whose voice doth pierce the velvet night,
A silver thread spun through the dark, a beacon 'gainst encroaching blight.
From boughs of oak and elder green, thy melody, a solace keen,
O Common Nightingale, thy song doth make the weary soul serene.*

## Physical Description

This modest songster, oft termed "Common" for its ubiquity, presenteth a countenance of humble grace.  Dressed in hues of brown and grey, a camouflage 'gainst twilight's embrace, it doth possess a compact frame, feathered sleekly save for a rust-red breast, a vibrant testament to its melodious heart.  Upon its slender legs, tipped with sharp claws, it perches with agility, a feathered jewel amidst the verdant tapestry.

## Habitat and Distribution

Throughout the realm's woodlands, meadows kissed by sun, and gardens where blossoms unfold, the Common Nightingale maketh its dwelling. From the fertile plains to woodland fringes, its presence speaks to a fondness for temperate climes.  A wanderer it is, yet ever tethered to locales boasting ample shelter and sustenance, a faithful companion to burgeoning life.
