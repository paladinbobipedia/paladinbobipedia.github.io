# Glove

*A hymn unto the humble Glove, shield of hand and ward against the biting frost.  A bastion 'gainst the cruel embrace of thorn and rending steel, thou art a silent sentinel, ever faithful in thy leathern keep.*

## Description

Glove, a garment oft overlooked save in direst need, doth adorn the hand, a supple carapace of woven hide or hardened metal.  Forged in craftmanship's fire or tanned from beastly hide, its purpose rings clear: to safeguard the dextrous appendage, protector of mace and quill alike. From the simple russet leather favoured by the peasant to the gleaming plates adorning a knight's gauntlet, Glove varies in construction to suit its wearer's station and the perils they face.

##  Purposes & Protections

Glove serves a manifold of needs, a veritable champion of the hand. Firstly, 'tis a bulwark against the elements, shielding delicate skin from wind's lash and winter's icy clutch.  Secondly, in the theatre of combat, Glove deflects the wicked thrust of blade or the savage blow of bludgeon.  Sharper implements find purchase hindered, whilst blunt force finds its fury tempered by the glove's resilient embrace. ##  Varieties & Craftsmanship

The realm boasts a tapestry of Glove designs, each reflecting purpose and artistry:

* **Leathern Gloves:** Common and versatile, crafted from tanned hides, suitable for labourers, travellers, and skirmishers.
* **Gauntlets:**  Favoured by warriors, these rigid protectors, sometimes of metal laced leather, offer superior defense, augmenting the wielder's striking power.
* **Splint Gauntlets:**  For the knights and champions, these marvels of interlocking plates provide unmatched shielding, resembling unto miniature armours for the hand.
* **Mage's Gloves:**  Woven with arcane threads or etched with warding runes, these gloves channel spells and safeguard against magical backlash.
* **Tinker's Gloves:**  Sturdy yet nimble, designed with reinforced fingertips for delicate manipulations and toolwork.

Thus, Glove, in its myriad forms, stands sentinel, a testament to human ingenuity and a silent guardian of countless hands throughout the land.
