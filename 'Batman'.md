# Batman

*Hark, a shadowed sentinel, a wraith of justice in Gotham's night!  His courage, a beacon 'gainst the encroaching gloom, his wisdom, a shield 'gainst folly's blight. Batman, he stands, a knight errant forged not in steel, but in unyielding will, a protector crowned not with gold, but righteous zeal.*

## Origins and Guise

Batman, known in the vulgar tongue as "Bruce Wayne," doth bear a tragic genesis.  A youth beset by darkness when fiends slew his progenitors 'fore his very eyes, he vowed vengeance upon all villainy.  Shunning conventional knighthood, he sculpted himself into a nocturnal warrior, a spectre cloaked in black, the Batsymbol emblazoned upon his breast a mark of fear unto the wicked. This guise, a carefully crafted artifice, permits him to strike terror into the hearts of evildoers whilst shielding his true identity, a fortress of secrecy amidst Gotham's decay.

## Arsenal and Acumen

Whilst lacking the Paladin's holy magic, Batman wields technology and training as potent weapons. His "gadgets," as common folk term them, are marvels of invention: grappling hooks for aerial maneuvers, armored suits deflecting blade and bullet, and a myriad of ingenious tools born from his intellect. More than muscle, he is a master strategist, a student of combat disciplines both ancient and arcane, his mind a labyrinth of deduction, ever predicting his foe's next move.
