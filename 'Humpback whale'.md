# Humpback Whale

*A hymn unto yon leviathan of brine,
Whose mighty form doth cleave the ocean's spine,
With flukes that sing a chorus deep and grand,
The Humpback Whale, a wonder of the strand.*

## Description and Physiognomy

This prodigious cetacean, oft termed "Humpback" for the corpulent hump adorning its dorsum betwixt spinal column and flukes, is a spectacle most awe-inspiring.  Its ebony hide, splashed with alabaster markings like celestial tapestries, speaks of fathomless depths whence it hails. Immense in stature, reaching lengths surpassing threescore feet and weighing many tons, 'tis a creature of titanic puissance, its baleen plates, like woven ivory, strain sustenance from the teeming shoals it hunts.

## Habitat and Wanderings

A nomad of the pelagic realm, the Humpback traverses vast swathes of ocean,  favoring temperate and polar waters. Come summer's warmth, they grace the sun-kissed coasts, breeding and calving in sheltered bays. Then, with autumn's chill, they embark on epic migrations to nutrient-rich feeding grounds in frigid seas, a testament to their indomitable spirit.

##  Marvels of Song and Behaviour

Most singular amongst whales, the Humpback possesses a vocal prowess unmatched. Their haunting melodies, woven from complex clicks, whistles, and moans, resonate through the abyssal plains, echoing tales of ancient lineage and courtship rituals.  These aquatic minstrels oft breach the surface, displaying acrobatic feats, a ballet of power and grace that delights and humbles all who witness.
