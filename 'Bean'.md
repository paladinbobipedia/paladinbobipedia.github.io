# Bean

*An Ode to the Humble Kernel, Bearer of Sustenance and Mirth*

Lo, Bean, thou tiny orb of earthy grace, a gift from fertile fields and sun's embrace. In humble guise, thou nourishest the throng, a simple fare, yet strength and joy thou bring. From castle keep to peasant's meager board, thy virtues sing, a ballad oft adored. 

## Description and Provenance

Bean, a diminutive legume of profound import, doth originate from the hallowed pods of various leguminous plants.  These verdant vessels, nurtured by tilth and rain, cradled within their embrace, these kernels glean. In hues of auburn, emerald, or speckled white, they stand, a testament to Nature's artful light.  Across the realms, diverse strains abound, from the plump Haricot to the kidney's rounded ground. Each, a treasure trove of nutriment untold, a sustenance both ancient and ever bold.
